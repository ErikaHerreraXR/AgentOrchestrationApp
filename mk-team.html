What Does This Mean?

Plain English Terms — click to expand

System Prompt ▼

The instructions you give your AI before it starts — like a job description handed to a new employee on day one.

LLM ▼

Large Language Model — the brain inside Claude and ChatGPT. Trained on enormous amounts of text so it can write, reason, and answer like a human.

Orchestration ▼

Coordinating multiple AI agents to work as a team — like a conductor keeping all musicians in sync.

Workflow ▼

Steps that happen in a specific order, automatically — like a recipe your AI follows every time without you re-explaining it.

Token ▼

How AI measures text — roughly 1 token = 1 word. AI tools charge or limit usage based on tokens.

API ▼

A connection between two apps — like a phone call between software. Buffer uses Instagram's API to auto-post for you.

Getting Started

Team Setup Checklist

Follow these 8 steps to get all 6 agents running in under one hour.

1

Open Claude.ai (free or Pro)

The free tier gets you started. Pro ($20/mo) unlocks more speed and messages — worth it once you're running daily.

2

Write down your brand details before copying anything

Prepare: brand name, your name, niche, target audience, brand voice (e.g., "bold and funny"), and your main platform focus.

3

Activate GUARDIAN first — set your quality standard

Always start with GUARDIAN. Open a new Claude.ai conversation, paste the prompt, and set your quality bar before anything goes out.

4

Set up NOVA — your content engine

Paste NOVA's prompt with your details. Test with: "NOVA, write me today's Instagram caption about [topic]." Refine until the voice sounds right.

5

Activate ARIA for scheduling

Connect ARIA alongside Buffer or Later. Test: "ARIA, give me this week's posting schedule and hashtag sets." You're live when you schedule your first post.

6

Save all 6 customized prompts in one Google Doc

Name it "METAKITTYZ Agent Library." This is your team's employee handbook. Guard it like the most important document in your business.

7

Run your first daily briefing

Go to the Command Center and use Command 1 (The Morning Audit). Ask GUARDIAN for your baseline score — everything improves from here.

8

Generate your first full content suite

Activate NOVA and PULSE. Ask for one post per platform + one video script for this week. Review with GUARDIAN. Schedule with ARIA. You're in business. 🎉
