Your Cheat Sheet — Plain English Glossary

Every term you'll see in this system, explained like you're talking to a person — not a textbook.

Hook

The first 1–3 seconds of content that stops someone from scrolling. If your hook doesn't grab attention instantly, nothing else matters.

CTA (Call to Action)

Telling your audience exactly what to do next — "Follow me", "Click the link", "Comment below". Always end your content with one.

Content Calendar

A schedule of what you're posting and when. Your AI team fills this for you — you just approve and hit publish.

Repurposing

Taking one piece of content and turning it into many. That's exactly what the 1→12 machine does — one idea becomes 12 posts.

Engagement Rate

The percentage of people who interact with your post (likes, comments, shares). Higher engagement = more organic reach from the algorithm.

Algorithm

The invisible system each platform uses to decide who sees your content. Your job: give it signals (engagement, watch time, saves) to reward.

Organic

Free reach — people finding your content without you paying for ads. Consistency + quality = organic growth over time.

Paid / Boosted

Content amplified with advertising spend. REACH agent handles your paid strategy — ad copy, targeting, budgets.

Impressions

How many times your content was shown — even to the same person, even without interaction. A leading indicator of reach.

Reach

How many unique people saw your content. Unlike impressions, each person counts only once regardless of how many times they viewed it.

UGC (User Generated Content)

Raw, authentic-feeling video content — looks like a real person filmed it on their phone. Performs extremely well in ads and organically.

B-Roll

Background footage that supports your main video — hands typing, products being used, lifestyle shots. Makes your content look polished.

Voiceover

Audio recorded separately from video. Common in AI avatar content — your AI persona speaks while the visual plays. Higgsfield handles this.

A/B Testing

Trying two versions of the same content to see which performs better. REACH agent automates this for your ads — different hooks, headlines, CTAs.

Funnel

The journey from stranger → follower → buyer. Your content moves people through this funnel. Top of funnel: awareness. Bottom: purchase.
