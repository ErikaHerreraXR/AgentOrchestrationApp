💡 What are prompt packs?

A prompt pack is a collection of pre-written instructions you paste into Claude AI (or ChatGPT) to get a specific result. Instead of figuring out the right words yourself, you use a prompt that's already been tested and proven to work. Think of it like a recipe — you follow the steps, and you get the dish.
