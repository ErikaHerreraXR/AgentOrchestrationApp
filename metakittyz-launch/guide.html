📖 Step-by-Step Build Guide

6 Episodes. 1 AI Business.

Complete each episode in order. Check off each step as you go. No coding. No experience needed. Just follow along.

01

Your AI Foundation

Set up Claude AI and write your Brand Bible

🐣
▼
You'll have: A Claude AI account + a Brand Bible document that tells your AI team exactly who you are and how to sound like you.
  • Go to claude.ai → click "Start for free" → sign in with Google or email
  • Start a new chat. Paste this: "My name is [NAME]. I create content about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]. My vibe is [casual / bold / professional]. Write my brand voice guide."
  • Copy the output into a Google Doc — title it "My Brand Bible." This is your most important document.
  • Ask Claude: "Using my brand voice guide above, write 3 versions of my bio for social media."
  • Pick your favorite version and update your Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn bio right now.
  • I created a Claude AI account
  • I wrote my Brand Bible
  • I updated my social media bios
02

Build Your AI Team

Create your 6 AI agents — NOVA, PULSE, ARIA, REACH, SPARK, GUARDIAN

🔧
▼
You'll have: 6 AI agents, each with a specific job. You tell them who they are once — then they remember it every time you chat with them.
  • Open Claude and start a new chat for your first agent, NOVA.
  • Paste: "You are NOVA, my AI Content Creator. [Paste your Brand Bible]. Your job is to write all my content in my brand voice. When I give you a topic, create: a hook, 3 key points, and a call to action."
  • Test NOVA by saying: "Write a post about why AI is the best tool for solo creators." See how it sounds like you.
  • Repeat for ARIA: "You are ARIA, my Social Media Manager. Adapt content I give you for Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and X. Match each platform's style and character limits."
  • Set up GUARDIAN: "You are GUARDIAN, my QA Agent. Review content for: FTC disclosure, brand voice consistency, and no false claims. Flag anything that needs fixing."
  • Build PULSE, REACH, and SPARK the same way — each gets their own chat with their job description + your Brand Bible.
  • NOVA — Content Creator is set up
  • ARIA — Social Media Manager is set up
  • GUARDIAN — QA Agent is set up
  • PULSE, REACH, and SPARK are set up
03

The 1→12 Content Machine

Turn one idea into 12 posts across 6 platforms in 30 minutes

🎨
▼
You'll have: A weekly content workflow where one topic becomes 12 ready-to-post pieces of content — your full week, done in under 30 minutes.
  • Choose ONE topic for the week. Example: "Why every creator needs an AI team in 2025."
  • Give it to NOVA: "Create a full content suite for this topic: [TOPIC]. I need: 1 YouTube script hook, 2 Instagram captions, 2 TikTok scripts, 2 LinkedIn posts, 2 X/Twitter posts, 2 email snippets, and 1 blog intro."
  • Take each piece and hand it to ARIA: "Adapt these for each platform — match the vibe, keep the message."
  • Run everything through GUARDIAN: "Review all this content for compliance and brand consistency."
  • Create a free account at buffer.com or later.com and paste the posts in to schedule them for the week.
  • Best posting times: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday — at 9am, 12pm, and 6pm in your local timezone.
  • I ran the 1→12 workflow for my first topic
  • I set up a Buffer or Later account
  • I have at least 3 posts scheduled this week
04

Grow Your Audience

AI-written ads and a $5/day campaign to grow your following

📢
▼
You'll have: Your first paid campaign running — REACH writes the copy, GUARDIAN checks it, and you launch it. Even $5/day can build real momentum.
  • Ask REACH: "Write 3 ad hooks under 10 words each for this offer: [your freebie or page]. Target: [your audience description]. Make them scroll-stopping."
  • Pick the best hook. Ask REACH to expand it: "Turn this into a full ad: headline, 2-sentence body, and a CTA button text."
  • Run the ad through GUARDIAN: "Check this ad for FTC compliance, false promises, and platform policy issues."
  • Go to Meta Business Suite (Facebook/Instagram ads) or TikTok Ads Manager — create a free account.
  • Create a campaign → pick Traffic or Awareness → paste your ad copy → set $5/day budget → set interest targeting that matches your audience → launch.
  • Check results after 3 days. If it's getting clicks, let it run. If not, ask REACH to write 3 new hooks and test again.
  • I wrote 3 ad hooks with REACH
  • I set up a Meta or TikTok Ads account
  • My first campaign is live
05

Sell Your Prompts

Package your best prompts as a digital product and make your first sale

💰
▼
You'll have: Your first digital product on sale. The prompts you've already built and tested — packaged so other creators can shortcut their learning curve.
  • Open your best Claude conversations and copy your top 5 prompts that got great results.
  • Open a Google Doc. For each prompt, write: the name, what it does in one sentence, the exact prompt text, and an example of the output you got.
  • Export the document as a PDF. Name it: "[Your Name] AI Starter Prompts"
  • Go to gumroad.com → Create an account → New Product → Digital Product → Upload your PDF → Set the price to $17.
  • Write a product description using NOVA: "Write a Gumroad product description for my AI prompt starter pack. It includes 5 proven prompts for [topic]. Audience: [your audience]. Price: $17."
  • Publish and share the link in your bio, a post, and anywhere you have community — your first sale is coming.
  • I packaged my top 5 prompts into a PDF
  • My product is live on Gumroad
  • I shared my product link at least once
06

Run Your AI Business

Set up automation so your business runs without you being online all day

🚀
▼
You'll have: A fully automated weekly content routine. 45 minutes of work on Monday morning = a full week of content running on its own.
  • Every Monday, spend 30 minutes with NOVA: pick your topic for the week, run the 1→12 workflow, schedule everything in Buffer.
  • Set up a free email list at beehiiv.com → use NOVA to write a 3-email welcome sequence for new subscribers.
  • Connect your product link to your email welcome sequence so every new subscriber sees your prompt pack.
  • Once a week, ask GUARDIAN to review your last 5 posts for brand consistency and FTC compliance.
  • Post your results publicly — share what's working, what you built, and what your AI team made this week. This IS your content.
  • Tag @metakittyz when you post your wins. You're part of the 1M mission now. 🚀
  • I set up my email list on Beehiiv
  • I have a weekly Monday content routine
  • I posted my "I built my AI business" update

Plain English Glossary

Terms You'll Actually Use

AI Agent
An AI with a specific job and personality. Like an employee who only does what their job description says.
System Prompt
The instructions you give an AI before it starts working. It tells the AI WHO it is and HOW to behave.
Brand Voice
The personality behind your words. Consistent across all content so people recognize it as you.
FTC Disclosure
A required legal statement for paid promotions and AI-generated content. "Powered by AI" or "#ad" — it's the law.
Content Pipeline
The path from idea → creation → review → scheduling → publishing. Your 1→12 machine is your pipeline.
Digital Product
Something you sell online that requires no shipping — like a PDF or prompt pack. Create once, sell forever.
Automation
Setting up tasks so they happen on their own without you clicking anything — like autopay for your content business.
Lead Magnet
A free gift you offer in exchange for someone's email address — a guide, cheat sheet, or free prompt pack.
Prompt
The instructions or question you give to an AI. Better prompts = better output. It's like knowing exactly what to wish for.
Hallucination
When AI makes up something that sounds true but isn't. Always fact-check before publishing — GUARDIAN's job is to catch this.
