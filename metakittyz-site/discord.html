# prompt-lab
Prompt Lab
Share prompts · Get feedback · Iterate together
Welcome to the lab. This is where prompts get made and broken. 🔬

Share a prompt you wrote. Ask why it's not working. Post the before AND after when you iterate — those transformations are gold. Request a review. Offer to review someone else's.

Here's my honest take: writing great prompts is a skill — and like every skill, it gets better with practice and feedback. The fastest way to level up is to share your work and let the community help. Nobody here is judging. We're all figuring it out together. Your "bad" prompt today is someone else's breakthrough tomorrow. Post it. 💚
📝
Share the full prompt — role, context, task, format, constraints. All 5 layers.
🔄
Share the output too — helps everyone understand what you're going for.
💡
Iterate publicly. Version 1 → Version 2 → Version 3. Show the process.
✅
Be specific with feedback. "The hook is weak" beats "this is bad."
