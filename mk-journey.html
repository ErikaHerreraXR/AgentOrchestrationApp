What I'm Learning — Shared Live
Enrolled in Harvard's 2.5-week AI Agentic Intensive. Every concept I learn, I turn into a reel for you.
Concept 01
"AI agents can collaborate like a specialized team — each one owns a single skill and passes the work forward."
This is why PI Agent OS uses 7 specialized agents instead of one general AI. Specialization = better output.
Concept 02
"The brief is the most powerful tool in agentic AI. Garbage in, garbage out — every time."
I built an entire briefing system for my platform because of this. A good brief eliminates 80% of revision rounds.
Concept 03
"Governance in AI isn't about restriction — it's about building systems you can trust at scale."
GUARDIAN agent in my team handles this. Quality control isn't overhead — it's the foundation of consistency.
Concept 04
"Multi-agent pipelines don't just automate tasks — they eliminate the coordination tax entirely."
The orchestration layer is where the magic happens. No more repeating yourself to 8 different people.
Concept 05
"The future of AI isn't one model that does everything — it's orchestrated specialists doing their best work."
Just like a world-class team. No single person is best at everything. Systems beat superheroes.
Concept 06
"Human-in-the-loop isn't a limitation — it's a competitive advantage when designed intentionally."
My platform keeps humans in control at every key decision point. AI does the work, humans set the direction.
