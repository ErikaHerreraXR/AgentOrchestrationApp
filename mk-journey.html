⚖️ BEFORE YOU LAUNCH

Legal & Compliance Checklist

Set this up before you hit 1,000 followers. Doing it early saves you major headaches later. Check off each item as you complete it.

⚖️ Before You Launch — Legal Checklist

These are not optional if you plan to monetize. Each item protects you and your business as you scale.

Register Your Business NameLLC or Sole Proprietorship — consult an attorney for what's right for your situation. Protects your personal assets.
Open a Separate Business Bank AccountNever mix personal and business funds. Makes taxes cleaner and looks more professional to partners.
Set Up FTC-Compliant Disclosure TagsUse #ad, #sponsored, or #AIGenerated on all commercial content. FTC requires this — non-compliance means fines.
Read Each Platform's Terms of Service for AI ContentTikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn each have AI content policies. Know them before you post at scale.
Purchase Music LicensingIf using music, you need a license. Epidemic Sound, Musicbed, or Artlist. Unlicensed music = content takedowns.
Get a Privacy Policy for Your WebsiteRequired by law if you collect emails or use cookies. Use Termly.io, Iubenda, or hire a lawyer to draft one.
Understand DMCA — Don't Use Others' Content Without PermissionCopyright law applies to your AI content too. Don't train models on others' IP or use copyrighted images.
Keep Records of AI-Generated ContentFor tax purposes, track which content was AI-assisted. This matters for deductions and audit readiness.
